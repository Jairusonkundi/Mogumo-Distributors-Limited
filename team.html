Clifford Barare-Founder and Director

Clifford Barare is a seasoned professional with 13 years of experience in business management and finance. Holding a Bachelor of Commerce degree and being a Certified Public Accountant (CPA), Barare brings a unique blend of theoretical knowledge and practical expertise to his roles as Chairman, Board Member, and Founder. With a strong foundation in business management, Barare l has successfully navigated various leadership positions, demonstrating proficiency in strategic planning, financial management, and corporate governance. His hands-on experience in overseeing organizational operations and financial performance has equipped him with the skills needed to drive sustainable growth and value creation. As Chairman and Founder, Barare provides strategic direction and guidance to the board, leveraging his expertise in financial analysis and risk management to make informed decisions that align with the company's objectives. He fosters a culture of transparency, accountability, and ethical conduct, ensuring that the organization operates in compliance with regulatory standards and best practices. As a Board member Barare, actively contributes to discussions and decision-making processes, drawing on his extensive experience to provide valuable insights and perspectives. He collaborates with fellow board members and executive leadership to identify opportunities for growth, mitigate risks, and enhance shareholder value. Barare's dedication to professional development and commitment to excellence make him a valuable asset to any organization. His combination of business acumen, financial expertise, and leadership skills position him as a trusted advisor and effective steward of corporate governance.
